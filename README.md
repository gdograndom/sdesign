Micro Processor:
Raspberry Pi 3B - Head Node

|               | Solenoid 9  | Solenoid 11  | Solenoid 16  |
|     :---:     |     :-:     |     :-:      |     :-:      |
| Direct Drive  |     LOW     |     LOW      |     HIGH     |
| Direct Charge |     HIGH    |     LOW      |     LOW      |
| Boost Drive   |     HIGH    |     HIGH     |     HIGH     |
| Regen Braking |     HIGH    |     LOW      |     LOW      |



Solenoid 9 = pin 31

Solenoid 11 = pin 33

Solenoid 16 = pin 35
